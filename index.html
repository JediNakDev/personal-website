Energy and exergy analysis with cost-efficiency comparison of modified phase change material (PCM) integrated with solar panel thermal system

@Kamnoetvidya Science Academy

This study investigates the efficiency and cost-effectiveness of integrating Phase Change Materials (PCMs) into solar panels to optimize power output and power conversion efficiency under varying temperature conditions. The simulation results demonstrate that the solar panel integrated with PCM generates up to 11.1% more power than a regular solar panel and the best cost-efficiency of the modified PCM is achieved, with an increase of up to 637.7%, using a combination of paraffin wax and polyethylene glycol.
